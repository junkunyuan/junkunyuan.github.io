[2024-05-16] Chameleon: Mixed-Modal Early-Fusion Foundation Models (arXiv 2024)

Authors: Chameleon Team

Organizations: FAIR at Meta

Summary: Early-fusion token-based mixed-modal model (34B) trained on 10T interleaved mixed-modal data for understanding & generation of text & images.

  • Pre-training data for the first stage. Text: data used to train LLaMa-2 and CodeLLaMa, 2.9T text-only tokens; Text-image: public + licensed data with 512x512 resized image, 1.4B text-image pairs, 1.5T text-image tokens; Text-image interleaved: public data, 400B tokens.
  • Pre-training data for the second stage. Lower the weight of the first stage by 50%; mix in higher quality data and instruction tuning data.
  • Architecture: follow LLaMa-2, RMSNorm, SwiGLU, RoPE, AdamW.
  • Fine-tuning strategy: balance data modalities, cosine lr schedule.
  • Evaluation: prompts from crowdsourcing;
